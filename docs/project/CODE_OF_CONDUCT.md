# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the Socrates community and its projects will experience an environment that is free from harassment and discrimination.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Helping others learn and grow
- Celebrating each other's successes

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Offensive comments related to gender, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, religion, or national origin
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Violent threats or language directed against another person
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to:

- Remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct
- Temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for behaviors that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful

## Reporting Guidelines

If you witness or experience any violation of this Code of Conduct, please report it to the maintainers:

- **Email:** conduct@socrates.io
- **GitHub:** Create a private security advisory report
- **Alternative:** Contact a community moderator

### What to Include in Your Report

- Your contact information
- Names (real, nicknames, or pseudonyms) of any individuals involved
- Description of the incident
- Where and when it occurred
- Any additional context you think is helpful

Reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. All reports will be kept confidential.

## Consequences

Violations of the Code of Conduct may result in:

1. **Warning:** Private written warning and clarification of the violation
2. **Temporary Ban:** Temporary removal from community participation
3. **Permanent Ban:** Permanent removal from the community

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project repositories and communities
- Public spaces where a community member is representing the project or community
- Private communications between community members about project/community business

## Exceptions

We recognize that some community members may have cultural or communication styles that differ from the default assumptions in this code. We welcome these differences and ask that all members attempt to understand and respect them.
